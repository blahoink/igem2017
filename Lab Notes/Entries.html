Plasmid Purification

Introduction

Slightly modified GeneJet protocol for purifying DNA

Materials

  • Binding Buffer
    • Wash Buffer
      • Elution Buffer
        • Spin Column
          • Falcoln Tube

          Procedure

          • Purify DNA
          1. Add 1:1 binding buffer directly to the PCR tube
          1. If PCR product < 500 bp, add 1:2 isopropyl alcohol
          1. Transfer the entire solution to a spin column
          1. Centrifuge 45-60 sec at 12000-13000 RPM. Discard flow-through
          1. Add 500 mL wash buffer
          1. Centrifuge 45-60 sec at 12000-13000 RPM. Discard flow-through
          1. Centrifuge dry 1 min at 12000-13000 RPM. Discard flow-through
          1. Add 30 uL elution buffer
          1. Transfer the top column to a Falcoln tube and centrifuge 1 min at 12000-13000 RPM.
          1. Discard spin column
